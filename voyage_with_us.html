Voyage With Us

Talofa lava and Afio Mai! We, O le Aiga Folau o Samoa, for Va'atele Gaualofa welcome you to come and voyage with us!

Are you ready for an adventure to remember? Are you ready to experience traditional Samoan sailing? Do you want to experience the ocean and all its wonders while traveling on a sustainable traditional Polynesian voyaging canoe? Then come and visit us on the beautiful Gaualofa! Our voyages include:

  • Whale-Watching Day Trips,
  • Transport to Pristine Snorkeling and Dive Locations,
  • Site Seeing Tours and
  • Inter-island Charters.

The voyaging schedule for 2013 will be updated soon. Please visit this page again soon to schedule a voyage with our Gaualofa crew. We have sailed on-board Gaualofa for approximately 30,000 nautical miles since 2009! We look forward to seeing you on our next voyage!
